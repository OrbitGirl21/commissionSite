✧･ﾟ: *✧･ﾟ:*Welcome to Orbit Illustrations*:･ﾟ✧*:･ﾟ✧

Hello, I'm Orbit! I've been an artist for twelve years, and have been a digital artist for three of those years. Some things that really inspire me to create is my imagination, games, Dungeons and Dragons, and even Anime! I've been creating original pieces recently, to start steering away from relying on fan-art for my creative process, and so that it'll be more connected to me ♡


I created this website to start reaching out with my commissions and artwork, and to start branding myself. I've been practicing and creating for a long time, and I finally feel good enough to start branching out and showing everyone what I've made. Welcome, and I hope you enjoy the growth of my art! Thank you ♡
